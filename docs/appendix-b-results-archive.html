Appendix B : Results Archive

The aim of this section is to compare the modelling results against historical data collected within Cockbrun Sound and surrounding waters (where available), with outputs of various generations of the CSIEM. The model performance in predicting a range of relevant variables such as salinity, temperature, nitrogen, phosphorus and total chlorophyll-a, are assessed with a set of statistical metrics, and other supporting analyses.

This appendix is in progres during the active development of the model and being updated with the CSIEM modelling as it evolves.
